Why are we collecting data?

At GIC, we aim to provide our students with the best possible academic experience.

Collecting data on student progress allows us to enhance the quality of education and the success of our students.

How is data collection beneficial?

By analyzing student data, we gain insights into the effectiveness of our curriculum.

This enables us to recognise trends and make informed decisions to our curriculum.

  • provide feedback to our teaching staff to facilitate necessary adjustments to their individual teaching style
  • revise our course content to ensure its relevance and alignment with current industry standards.
